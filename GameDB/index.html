How To Play

Getting started is easy - follow these simple steps to begin your journey in the Dev vs Byte world.

1

Choose Your Side

Decide whether you want to play as a Developer building and protecting apps or a Hacker finding and exploiting vulnerabilities. Each offers a unique gameplay experience.

Complete the introductory tutorial to understand the game mechanics specific to your chosen side. Developers will learn about building apps and security, while Hackers will master exploitation techniques.
